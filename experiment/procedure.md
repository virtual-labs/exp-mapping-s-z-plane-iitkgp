### Procedure

<b>Steps to perform the simulation</b>


<ol type="1">

<li>At first enter the desired value of sampling time. Here default value is 1 sec for sampling time.</li></br>

<li>Click on 'Select Entry Method' dropdown button and select the available options to enter the values.</li></br>
<div align="center">
<img class="img-fluid"  src="./images/PZmap1.png" alt=""><br>        
<b> Fig.1. Select Entry Method dropdown button </b>						  
</div><br/>

<li>For Pole - Zero entry: click on 'Add Poles' button to enter the pole values and click on 'Add Zeros' button to enter the zero values.
Click 'Add Pole' or/and 'Add Zero' buttons multiple times to enter multiple pole/zero values. </li><br/>
<div align="center">
<img class="img-fluid"  src="./images/PZmap2.png" alt=""><br>
<b> Fig.2. Add Poles and Add Zeros buttons</b>						  
</div><br/>
<div align="center">
<img class="img-fluid"  src="./images/PZmap3.png" alt=""><br>
<b> Fig.3. Entry of poles and zeros values</b>						  
</div><br/>

<li> Click on 'Plot' button to get the Pole-Zero map of S plane and Z plane.
Plot can be downloaded by clicking on 'Download' button.</li><br/>


<li>Click on 'Enter &zeta; , &omega;<sub>n</sub> Values' dropdown button. 
Enter the values of damping ratio (&zeta;)and natural frequency (&omega;<sub>n</sub>).
Click on 'Run' button in the dropdown-menu to get the Pole Map of S plane and Z plane.
Plot can be downloaded by clicking on 'Download' button.</li><br/>
<div align="center">
<img class="img-fluid"  src="./images/PZmap4.png" alt=""><br> 
<b> Fig.4. Enter &zeta; , &omega;<sub>n</sub> values </b>						 
</div><br/>

<li>Click on 'Clear' button before every new simulation to clear the previous map.</li><br/>

<li><b>Note:</b> Do not click on 'Clear' button if you want to enter values of multiple poles and zeros.</li><br/>

</ol>
